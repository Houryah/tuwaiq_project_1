SAUDI GENOME PROGRAM

The Saudi Genome Program (SGP) will create a database to document the first genetic map of the Saudi society, develop personalized medicine practice, reduce the cost of healthcare, and improve quality of life. The program supports healthcare provision through better diagnosis, therapy, and prevention. Major outcomes will include the implementation of premarital and newborn screening, cancer screening and targeted therapy, and better management of other multifactorial genetic diseases such as diabetes and loss of vision/hearing.
